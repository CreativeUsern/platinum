First, backup the two "GraphiteAppearance.car" and "SystemAppearance.car" from the alias into a ~/backups folder or something. Then copy the two given files there, choose Graphite appearance from the System settings, then log out and log back in or just killall your finder and close and re-open all of your applications. To further modify these files to you liking, check out https://github.com/alexzielenski/ThemeEngine.
